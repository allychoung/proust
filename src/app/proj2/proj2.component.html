II. Writing

ENGW 3315, Spring 2023

From an early age, it was ingrained in me, and probably many others, that for a student to choose to be an English major, they have to want to be an English major. Perhaps, with this notion lurking in the back of my mind throughout my college life, my disdain, and even hatred, for writing snowballed into something greater. Struggling at a practice that is such a fundamental part of my choice of study felt like a blaring sign that I had made a mistake in my decision.
